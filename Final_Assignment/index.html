For Over 20 Years Oday's Outdoor Paintball has provided safe, fun and affordable Outdoor Paintball experience. We are Toronto's Best and Brightest Outdoor Paintball field. We are located in a the heart of Toronto at 401 and Dixon Road easily accessible using public transit. Oday's Outdoor Paintball is a Urban combat style with two story buildings and a great web of hiding spots and passage way's that let the players feel like they are in thick of action no matter where they are on the field.
